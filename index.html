Manask Psychotherapy Collective

‘Manask’ in Sanskrit means
“to say what is in one's heart”

Members of the Manask Psychotherapy Collective have come together because fundamentally they adhere to the same philosophy -  belief in the power of a safe therapeutic relationship, trust that everyone can heal and grow, see attunement as more important than theoretical orientation, seek supervision and continual training, respect diversity, and maintain high standards of ethical and professional practice.   

The members have different life and training experiences, varied interests and their fee structure and availability may not be the same as it is based on where they are in their professional journeys.
